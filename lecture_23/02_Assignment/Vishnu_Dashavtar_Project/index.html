Informaion About Lord Vishnu

Lord Vishnu is one of the principal deities in Hinduism, known as the preserver and protector of the universe. He is part of the holy trinity (Trimurti) along with Brahma (the creator) and Shiva (the destroyer). Vishnu is often depicted with a blue complexion, symbolizing his infinite nature. He is known for his ten avatars (Dashavatara), which include well-known incarnations such as Rama and Krishna, each embodying specific virtues and fulfilling divine purposes. Vishnu's followers, known as Vaishnavas, worship him for guidance and protection. His consort is Goddess Lakshmi, the goddess of wealth and prosperity. Vishnu's teachings emphasize dharma (righteousness) and the importance of devotion (bhakti) in spiritual practice. Through his avatars, he restores cosmic order and helps humanity overcome evil.
